How to play BeeHive on PC and Phone?

Anthony Lapid

Sep 21, 2019·7 min read

How to play BeeHive on PC?

⚜️Game start

🎯To start the game you should:

✅ Enter the address https://tronhives.com (please be sure to use https:// safe connection)

✅ You must have TronLink browser extension installed. Registration instructions of TronLink are here.

✅ You need to top up your wallet with TRX coins, by transferring them out from your other account or cryptocurrency exchange or by using one of the offered services for buying TRX on our site — https://tronhives.com/buy_trx_easy/

✅ Press Play button (start), after that you will see the game starting window, and if at the bottom of the window there is enabled Start button, then you need to choose the sponsor at the page — https://tronhives.com/representatives/ (you can not start the game without a sponsor). Also you can click at the title Representatives and you will be redirected to this page.

✅ At the representatives page click the button Choose a sponsor and you will be redirected to the game start window with filled Sponsor field.

✅ Press Start button and confirm the transaction in Tronlink. If after Tronlink transaction confirmation the page has not refreshed and you still see the game start window, just refresh the page manually again.

📱If you play from a smartphone, then here you are!

⚜️ Personal account

🎯 Balance replenishing

✅ To start playing you must buy the Wax which is required to buy bees and open the frames. The purchase of wax proseeds after clicking the Top up button.

✅ After that in the dialog window enter the amount of TRX which you want to put on your balance, click the Top up button and confirm this operation in Tronlink!

⚠️ Attention! There should be TRX coins on your Tronlink balance as they are used to confirm all the game transactions. We recommend to have at least 10 TRX on your balance.

⚠️ If you had confirmed the operation in Tronlink, but there is no changes on the page, then just refresh the page manually.

⚜️ Purchase of bees:

⭐️ The first AirDropBee frame is available for free for you! And you can gather 100 of wax from it every 8 hours. As the gathering wax operation also needs confirmation from Tronlink, don’t forget to have small amount of TRX on your balance.

⭐️ SmartBee purchase — enter the button Buy a Bee and if you have enough resources for buying you will be able to proceed the purchase, in the other case the button will be unavailable and you will see the Error message about low balance. You can use either wax or honey, notice that honey is used when the amount of wax is insufficiently.

⭐️ After buying all the bees in the frame the next frame with bees will be available for you. It must be unlocked first and after it’s unlocking you will get the first bee for free and will be able to buy further bees.

⭐️There is two frames with special requirements:

  • for TronBee purchase you must reach the Platinum medal;
  • the SuperBee is available when the smartcontract balance declines more than 25 percent from its maximum value, it doesn’t require unlocking, it can be bought by any willing person. When the smartcontract’s balance increases again, this bee is blocked up automatically.

⚜️ Reaching medals:

⭐️You must make points for reaching medals.

Action points are accumulated when you are buying wax and when your referrals make purchases. Each medal except for the first one gives you certain reward which is some amount of wax.

⚜️ Page Bee-garden:

⭐️ Here you can get your referral link for inviting other players into the game. Important — you will get 10% as honey out from all wax purchases by your referrals.

⭐️Also here you can see the list of all your referrals and the referral reward which you are getting from them.

⚜️ Page Events:

⭐️Here you can see your transactions of wax purchases and honey sale. Some operations can be displayed with delay as their confirmation in the network requires some time.

⚜️ Page Rating:

⭐️Here you can see the current players rating in the game. The rating is counted by the sum of wax and honey gathering per hour!

⚜️ Accrual of profit:

⭐️Profit gains automatically on your account every several seconds. You can use your accumulated resources any time you want to purchase bees for wax or withdraw honey from the game to your wallet.

⚜️ Production improvement:

⭐️Your bees bring you wax and honey, starting correlation of which is 40% to 60%. You can change it to get higher honey gathering percent up to 50% to 50%. To make an upgrade you must have HONEY on your balance.

♻️ If something in your personal account isn’t displayed correctly by your opinion — just refresh the page!

📱How to play BeeHive on your smartphone?

⭐️ To start playing on your 📱smartphone you need:

📲 Install one of the wallets on your phone: TronWallet, GuildChat, TronLink (only for Android), MathWallet.

💡Installation and registration instructions in TronWallet are here.

✳️Make sure that in the multiwallets variants (GuildChat, MathWallet) you have chosen Tron network.

✳️You must have TRX coins on your balance. Transfer them out from your other account or cryptocurrency exchange or by using one of the offered services for buying TRX on our site — https://tronhives.com/buy_trx_easy/

✳️Enter the site https://tronhives.com using built dapp-browser (please be sure to use safe connection https://)

✳️Press Play button (start), after that you will see the game starting window, and if at the bottom of the window there is enabled Start button, then you need to choose the sponsor at the page — https://tronhives.com/representatives/ (you can not start the game without a sponsor). Also you can click at the title Representatives and you will be redirected to this page.

🛑If error about Tronlink or Tronpay installation is displayed, ♻️📲 restart your wallet on your phone.

💡Press Start button and confirm the transaction in Tronlink. If after Tronlink transaction confirmation the page is not ♻️refreshed but you still see the game start window, ♻️just refresh the page manually, using the appropriate icon in the dapp-browser window.

♻️ If something in your personal account isn’t displayed correctly by your opinion — just refresh the page!

Possible problems in BeeHive

⚙️ In the case of our site — ⚜️ https://tronhives.com isn’t available, you can use mirror — ⚜️ https://tonhives.com. It is another physical server, which is situated in another region and if both of them are unavailable for you, the problem can be in some of the follows:

⁉️you have no stable internet connection — you can check using this site https://www.speedtest.net/

⁉️you need to clean your browser cache — use the combination of CTRL + SHIFT + DELETE

⁉️firewall is blocking your internet connection or some sites — check your firewall settings and add our game site to the list of exceptions

⭐️ If the site is available ⁉️ but you can not enter your account , there can be such problems:

🛑You haven’t logged into your wallet.

✅Solution — log in to your wallet or refresh the data in it.

🛑Cache has not been cleaned.

✅ For solution — i the Chrome browser use combination CTRL + SHIFT + DELET

🛑There are other extensions working which use web 3.0 games or use Tronlink.

✅ For the solution — turn off other browser extensions and close browser pages with other games opened. Re-log in TronLink and refresh the page of our game site.

🛑Your firewall is blocking your internet connection or some sites.

✅ For the solution — add our site https://tronhives.com to the firewall exceptions.

🛑Tron network error or Tronlink extension error.

✅For the solution — log out and then log in to your wallet one more time. If it doesn’t help wait for some time and repeat your try!

⁉️ If any of these actions haven’t brought result, connect for help!
